About

Consistent learner, hands-on builder.

The short version of how I got here, what I'm optimizing for, and how I like to work.

I'm a 3rd-year B.Tech Data Science student at Chalapathi Institute of Technology in Andhra Pradesh, India. I split my time between full-stack web development — HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB — and the data science side of my degree: Python, SQL, exploratory data analysis, and machine learning with scikit-learn.

My goal right now is a software engineering or full-stack development internship where I can work on production code, get code-reviewed by people better than me, and keep building the DSA and system-design fundamentals I'm still filling in.

I learn best by shipping something end-to-end, breaking it, and fixing it — most of what's in the projects section below started as "let me just try this" rather than a course assignment.

  • Comfortable across the MERN stack and Python-based ML tooling
  • Ships working, deployed projects — not just notebooks that run locally
  • Currently deepening DSA, Spring Boot, and database design
  • Direct communicator who moves quickly from idea to implementation
